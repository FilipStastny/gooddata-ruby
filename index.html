The Story

Since its beginning, GoodData has stuck to its mission of simplifying business intelligence and delivering real solutions to the widest audience. While GoodData has delivered the tools, processes, and platform to deliver end-to-end solutions that are much easier to implement, the process of building ETL is still complex and, at times, hard.
BAM! greatly simplifies the process of building ETL graphs. Using predefined, modular units, an ETL developer can rapidly assemble production-grade ETL that follows GoodData's recommended practices. The BAM! toolkit simplifies the process so that you can focus on what matters: delivering value-added reporting solutions to your customer.
